# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the AI-Native Technical Textbook on Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics. This comprehensive guide is designed for advanced students, engineers, and builders who want to understand and implement embodied intelligence systems.

## What is Physical AI?

Physical AI represents a paradigm shift from purely digital AI systems to AI that operates under real-world physical constraints. Unlike traditional AI that processes abstract data, Physical AI must navigate the complexities of physics, sensor limitations, actuator dynamics, and environmental uncertainties.

### Key Concepts in Physical AI

- **Embodied Intelligence**: Intelligence that emerges from the interaction between an agent and its physical environment
- **Sim-to-Real Transfer**: The challenge of transferring behaviors learned in simulation to real-world robots
- **Perception-Action Loops**: Continuous cycles of sensing, reasoning, and acting in dynamic environments
- **Multi-Modal Integration**: Combining visual, auditory, tactile, and proprioceptive information
